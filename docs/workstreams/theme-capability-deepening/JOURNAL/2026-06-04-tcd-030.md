## Implementation

- Added a renderer-owned `chart_palette` module.
- Moved XyChart plot palette parsing and plot-index color cycling out of `xychart.rs`.
- Kept resolved Mermaid `themeVariables.xyChart.plotColorPalette` authoritative.
- Added fallback derivation for missing non-default chart palettes from `xyChart.accentColor` or
  `primaryColor`, with the same HSL family-shift behavior as the `beautiful-mermaid` reference.
- Left `merman-core` theme expansion unchanged.

## Follow-Up

The new helper is suitable as the renderer-side chart palette seam. Mindmap, GitGraph, Radar, and
other palette-heavy families should only adopt it after their Mermaid-specific palette contracts are
reviewed, because several of those families intentionally derive colors from `cScale*` or their own
diagram-specific theme variables.
